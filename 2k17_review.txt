# Top five catchphrases that has dominated this year


 1 - Very tasty (pronounced "veli days-di")
 2 - Very sexy (pronounced "veri sae-sie")
 3 - Very nice (pronounced "veri rice")
 4 - The Chinaman (pronounced "duh chinaman")
 5 - Coconut Milk (pronounced "coconut milk", these words must be said after triple helpings of masala)

 6 - Please (pronounced "prhease")
 7 - Cute Biu (pronounced "cuhe bhiu")
 8 - Pooh (pronounced "PAOHHH")
 9 - No (pronounced "Noahhoowwwooohhhhh")
10 - The big one (pronounced "duh beeg wan")
